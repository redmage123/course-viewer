# TechFlow Solutions - Employee Handbook

## Welcome to TechFlow Solutions

### Our Mission
To empower businesses with innovative technology solutions that drive growth, efficiency, and digital transformation.

### Our Values
1. **Innovation First** - We embrace new ideas and technologies
2. **Customer Success** - Our clients' success is our success
3. **Collaboration** - Great things happen when we work together
4. **Integrity** - We do the right thing, always
5. **Growth Mindset** - We learn, adapt, and improve continuously

### Company History
TechFlow Solutions was founded in 2015 in Dublin, Ireland. What started as a 5-person consultancy has grown to over 200 employees across Ireland and the UK. We specialize in:
- Cloud architecture and migration
- Custom software development
- Data analytics and AI solutions
- IT managed services

## IT & Security

### Password Policy
- Minimum 14 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, number, and symbol
- Change every 90 days
- No password reuse (last 12 passwords)
- Use company password manager (1Password)

### Acceptable Use
- Company devices are for business use primarily
- Limited personal use acceptable
- No installation of unauthorized software
- Report any security incidents immediately
- Complete annual security awareness training

### Data Classification
- **Public**: Marketing materials, job postings
- **Internal**: Company policies, org charts
- **Confidential**: Client data, financial reports
- **Restricted**: PII, credentials, trade secrets

## Emergency Procedures

### Fire Safety
- Know your floor's fire exits
- Assembly point: Grand Canal Square (east side)
- Fire wardens on each floor (see notice boards)
- Do not use lifts during evacuation
- Monthly fire drills

### First Aid
- First aiders on each floor
- First aid kits in kitchen areas
- AED located at reception and Floor 2
- Emergency: Call 999, then notify reception (Ext. 0)

### Incident Reporting
Report all incidents via:
- Email: safety@techflowsolutions.ie
- Urgently: Reception (Ext. 0) or your manager
- Anonymous: Physical suggestion boxes on each floor
