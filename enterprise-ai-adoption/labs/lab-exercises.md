# Day 1 Labs: Foundations & Mindset Shift

## Lab 1.1: Workflow Autopsy

### Objective
Map a current workflow and identify friction points where AI might help.

### Instructions

**Step 1: Choose a Workflow**
Select a workflow you perform regularly (at least weekly). It should:
- Take at least 30 minutes to complete
- Involve multiple steps
- Have clear inputs and outputs

**Step 3: Identify Friction**

- **MH** = Manual Handoff
- **DD** = Duplicate Data Entry
- **IH** = Information Hunting
- **FT** = Format Translation
- **RB** = Review Bottleneck
- **RL** = Rework Loop

**Step 4: Calculate Totals**
- Total steps: _____
