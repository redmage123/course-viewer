# TechFlow Solutions - HR Policies & Procedures

## Leave & Time Off

### Annual Leave
- All full-time employees are entitled to 25 days annual leave per year
- Leave must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance for periods longer than 3 days
- Maximum 10 consecutive days without manager approval
- Unused leave can carry over up to 5 days to the following year

### Sick Leave
- Employees receive 10 paid sick days per year
- A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days
- Notify your manager before 9:00 AM on the first day of absence
- Extended sick leave (beyond 10 days) is handled through HR on a case-by-case basis

### Parental Leave
- Primary caregiver: 26 weeks paid leave
- Secondary caregiver: 4 weeks paid leave
- Must notify HR at least 8 weeks before expected start date
- Flexible return-to-work arrangements available

## Remote Work Policy

### Eligibility
- All employees who have completed their 6-month probation period
- Role must be suitable for remote work (determined by department head)
- Must have reliable internet connection and suitable home office setup

### Guidelines
- Maximum 3 remote days per week (Hybrid model)
- Core hours: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM (must be available)
- Weekly team meetings must be attended in-person
- Monthly all-hands meeting in office is mandatory

### Equipment
- Company provides laptop and monitor for home use
- EUR 500 one-time home office setup allowance
- IT support available remotely via helpdesk

## Expense Policy

### Travel Expenses
- Economy class for flights under 4 hours
- Business class for flights over 4 hours (with pre-approval)
- Hotel cap: EUR 150/night (Dublin), EUR 120/night (regional Ireland)
- Meal allowance: EUR 50/day for overnight travel

### Reimbursement Process
1. Submit expenses within 30 days of incurring them
2. Attach all receipts (photos acceptable)
3. Use the expense portal: expenses.techflowsolutions.ie
4. Approval within 5 business days
5. Reimbursement in next pay cycle

### Training & Development
- EUR 1,500 annual training budget per employee
- Conferences require manager pre-approval
- Professional certifications: 50% company funded

## Performance Reviews

### Schedule
- Quarterly check-ins with direct manager
- Annual comprehensive review in December
- Mid-year review in June for goal adjustment

### Process
1. Self-assessment submission (2 weeks before review)
2. Manager assessment
3. 360 feedback (for senior roles)
4. Review meeting
5. Development plan creation

## Code of Conduct

### Workplace Behaviour
- Treat all colleagues with respect and professionalism
- Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination
- Report concerns to HR or use anonymous ethics hotline
- Dress code: Business casual (client meetings: business formal)

### Confidentiality
- Do not share company information externally without authorization
- Client data must be handled per GDPR requirements
- Sign NDA for all project work
- Return all company property upon leaving
