### Exercise 2.5: Ethical Considerations Checklist

Before deploying AI in your marketing, review this checklist:

**Data Privacy:**
- [ ] No PII sent to public AI tools
- [ ] Data handling complies with GDPR/privacy regulations
- [ ] Customer consent obtained for AI-powered communications
- [ ] Using enterprise-grade tools for sensitive data

**Transparency:**
- [ ] Clear disclosure when using AI chatbots
- [ ] AI-generated content reviewed by humans
- [ ] No deceptive practices (fake reviews, fake testimonials)
- [ ] AI-generated images labeled when required

**Quality Control:**
- [ ] Human review process in place
- [ ] Fact-checking for AI-generated claims
- [ ] Brand voice consistency verified
- [ ] Bias monitoring in place
